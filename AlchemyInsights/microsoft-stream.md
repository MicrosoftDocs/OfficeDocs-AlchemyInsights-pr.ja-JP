- [Stream サービスの更新情報](https://techcommunity.microsoft.com/t5/microsoft-stream-service-updates/bd-p/StreamAnnouncements)
- [Microsoft Stream のグループとチャネル](https://docs.microsoft.com/stream/groups-channels-organization)
